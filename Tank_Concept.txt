Tank Battle 

< Concept >

Open world head-to-head tank combat game

Terrain will be used for tactical advantage

Focus will be on flow and feel

< Rules >

Can move anywhere in the terrain which is surrounded by mountains

Both players start with finite health and ammo

Each direct hit takes away health

The last player standing wins

< Requirements >

SFX: gun firing, explosions, barrel moving, turret moving, engine sound

Static Mesh: simple tanks comprising of track, turrets, body and barrel.

Textures: for visual flare

Music: background for tension

< Iteration >

World > Tank > Controls > Player 2 > UI > World
